About Us!


Opteez Ghana Limited is a fast growing Telecommunications/IT service provider that supports relevant industry players in the delivery of comprehensive managed services in the areas of Network Operations, Field Maintenance and Radio Access Network Planning and Optimization for increasing clients’ value.

OUR SERVICES

What we offer


Transmission Design/Planning and Implementation

  • Design and implement microwave point to point networks: long or short haul.
  • Evaluate loss/attenuation, fading and fading margins, quality, availability and interference analysis.
  • Microwave Link engineering.

Radio Network Design & Planning

  • RF network planning and design.
  • Site survey and validation.
  • Link budget analysis, design criteria and traffic analysis.
  • System dimensioning for coverage and capacity.

Radio network Optimization

  • System setting and test plan development.
  • Initial optimization testing of installed networks.
  • Functional testing and optimization for implemented sites.
  • Network drive test, call quality test and data analysis and reporting.


Operation and Maintenance

  • Site revisions, expansion & reconfiguration, Equipment Swap.
  • Trouble Ticketing and reporting
  • Corrective Maintenance (onsite support)
  • Preventive Maintenance of site infrastructure.

What we have done


Our vast expertise has afforded us the opportunity to execute projects for underlisted telecom players and we seek to expand our portfolio as we grow as a company

2G/3G Coverage and Quality Tuning for Tigo via Reime

The project was named ‘’PROJECT ELEVATE’’ and the primary aim was to reduce interference and eliminate pilot pollution. For this project scope, we carried out cluster drive test for about 400 sites in the capital city of Accra, Ghana.

Benchmark Drive for Ericsson

On this project, we performed drive tests for about 600 sites in the two largest cities of Ghana, Accra and Kumasi to ascertain coverage and quality of Airtel network against 4 other competitors’ networks (Vodafone, MTN, Tigo and Glo). We also proposed recommendations for physical optimization and implemented them as approved by the customer

LTE NW Tuning for Busy GH LTD

The scope of this project was for initial tuning, model tuning, coverage tuning, and quality improvement of 80 eNode Bs. After integration of the new sites, we carried out SSV drives test to ascertain the footprints/ coverage and did further tuning in terms of parameters, coverage and planning models
